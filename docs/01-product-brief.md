# 01 — Product Brief

## 1. Product summary

The app helps pet owners and caretakers manage everyday pet care. It combines pet profiles, reminders, health records, budget tracking, photos/memories, sitter booking, and AI support.

## 2. Target users

### Primary users

- Pet owners who want to manage health records, reminders, and costs.
- Busy owners who forget feeding, medication, grooming, vaccination, or checkup schedules.
- Owners who want one safe place to store pet information and medical images.

### Secondary users

- Pet lovers who want to share pet photos.
- People offering pet sitting services.
- Families/caretakers who may later share responsibility for pet care.

## 3. Core user problems

1. Pet information is scattered across notes, phone gallery, clinic papers, and memory.
2. Owners forget vaccination, medication, grooming, or feeding schedules.
3. Medical records and images are hard to find during vet visits.
4. Pet expenses are unclear and unmanaged.
5. Owners want fast guidance when something seems wrong, but need safe triage and vet guidance.
6. Finding pet sitters requires trust and communication.

## 4. Phase 1 value proposition

> Manage your pet's daily care, health history, expenses, memories, and AI care support in one app.

## 5. Feature pillars

### Pet Management

Central profile for each pet. All other data should connect to pets when possible.

### Reminder

Retention engine. Helps users return to the app through real care tasks.

### Medical Records

Trust and long-term value. Should become `Pet Health Vault`.

### Budget Statistics

Pet-specific expense tracking and insights.

### Photos Social

Emotional engagement. Public/private photos and memories.

### Doctor AI / Pet Care AI

Premium-value assistant. Should be safe, personalized, and backend-controlled.

### Sitter Booking

Lightweight connection platform. Marketplace infrastructure later.

### Subscriptions

Free/Premium tiers with practical limits and clear value.

### Settings

Account, subscription, notifications, appearance, language, support, logout.

## 6. Recommended positioning

Avoid positioning as:

```txt
A full social network for pets
A replacement for veterinary doctors
A full marketplace from day one
```

Prefer positioning as:

```txt
A personal pet care assistant for daily care, health records, reminders, and AI support.
```

## 7. MVP success metrics

Track:

- Number of pets created per user
- Reminder creation rate
- Reminder completion rate
- Medical records created
- Budget transactions created
- AI messages per active user
- Paywall view to conversion rate
- Free-to-premium conversion rate
- 7-day and 30-day retention
- Sitter profiles created
- Booking requests created
